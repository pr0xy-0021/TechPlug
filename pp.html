Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

TechPlug ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our tech services in Calgary, including computer repairs, website building, and other tech support services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Request our tech services (computer repair, website building, etc.)
  • Contact us via phone, email, or our website
  • Schedule an appointment or get a quote
  • Sign up for our newsletter or promotions

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Business information (for website services)
  • Payment details (processed securely by our payment processors)
  • Details about your tech issues or project requirements

Technical Information

For repair and support services, we may collect:

  • Device information (make, model, specifications)
  • Diagnostic data about your tech issues
  • System logs and error reports (with your permission)

For Website Building Services: When we create websites for clients, we may process business information, images, and other content you provide solely for the purpose of building your website. This content remains your property.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Other standard web analytics data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our tech services
  • Diagnose and repair your devices
  • Build and maintain websites for clients
  • Communicate with you about your service requests
  • Process payments and send invoices
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send service-related notices and updates
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information in these situations:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist us in providing services (e.g., payment processors, web hosting for client sites)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger or sale of assets

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure storage of physical and digital records
  • Encryption for sensitive data
  • Limited access to personal information

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@techplug.ca.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services you've requested
  • Maintain business records for legal and accounting purposes
  • Comply with applicable laws

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted on our website with a new "Last Updated" date.
